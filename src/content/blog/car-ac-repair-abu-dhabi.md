In Abu Dhabi, a car without a working AC is almost undriveable between April and October. At [Al Sham Palace Auto Car Service](/), our AC repair team fixes dozens of vehicles every week during summer. Here are the most common reasons your car AC stops cooling and what we do to fix them.

## Reason 1: Low AC Refrigerant (Gas)

The most common cause of poor cooling is a refrigerant leak. Modern cars use R134a or R1234yf gas. Over time, small leaks from seals or hoses reduce the gas level, reducing cooling efficiency drastically.

**The fix**: Our technicians use a leak detector to find the source, repair the leak, then refill the refrigerant to the correct level. This typically takes one to two hours.

## Reason 2: Faulty AC Compressor

The compressor is the heart of the AC system. When it fails, the AC produces warm air no matter how low you set the temperature. You may also hear a clicking or clunking sound from the engine bay.

**The fix**: We diagnose the compressor with an AC pressure test and electronic scan. A seized compressor needs replacement. We stock compressors for all major car brands.

## Reason 3: Blocked or Dirty Condenser

The condenser sits at the front of the car behind the bumper. In Abu Dhabi, dust and sand clog the condenser fins, reducing heat exchange efficiency.

**The fix**: A professional condenser clean restores efficiency. This is recommended as part of every annual AC service for UAE vehicles.

## Reason 4: Clogged Cabin Air Filter

A blocked cabin filter restricts airflow from the AC vents. If you notice weak airflow even when the AC seems to run, the cabin filter is often the cause.

**The fix**: Cabin filter replacement is quick and affordable — often done in under 30 minutes. We recommend replacing it every six months in Abu Dhabi.

## Reason 5: Faulty Blower Motor

The blower motor circulates the cooled air into the cabin. When it fails, you get no airflow from the vents regardless of how the system is set.

**The fix**: We test the blower motor electrically and replace it if faulty. Our [auto electrical team](/services/auto-electrical-works/) handles all blower motor repairs.

## Reason 6: Refrigerant Expansion Valve or Orifice Tube Failure

These components regulate refrigerant flow. A stuck or blocked valve causes the AC to freeze up intermittently or blow warm air at random.

**The fix**: These are diagnosed with AC manifold gauges and replaced if faulty. Our technicians are trained for all modern AC systems.

## When to Call for AC Repair in Abu Dhabi

Do not wait for a complete failure. Contact us if you notice:

- Weak cooling even at maximum settings
- Warm air from vents
- Unusual smell from the AC (could be mould in the evaporator)
- Water dripping inside the car
- Unusual noise when the AC is on
