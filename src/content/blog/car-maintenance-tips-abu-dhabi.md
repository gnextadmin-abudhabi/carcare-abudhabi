Abu Dhabi's climate is one of the toughest in the world for vehicles. With summer temperatures exceeding 45°C and a dusty desert environment, your car faces challenges that drivers in cooler climates never deal with. At [Al Sham Palace Auto Car Service](/), we have been helping Abu Dhabi car owners since 1999, and here are our top ten maintenance tips.

## 1. Service Your Car AC Before Summer

The car AC system works harder than almost any other component in Abu Dhabi. Before summer arrives, have the AC gas level checked, the cabin filter replaced, and the condenser cleaned. A well-maintained AC also improves fuel efficiency. Learn more about our [Car AC Repair service in Abu Dhabi](/services/car-ac-repair/).

## 2. Check Tyre Pressure Weekly

Heat causes tyre pressure to rise. An over-inflated tyre wears unevenly and is more prone to blowouts. Check your tyre pressure every week during summer, ideally in the morning before the car has been driven. For professional tyre service across Abu Dhabi, visit our [Tyre Services page](/services/tyre-services/).

## 3. Change Engine Oil on Schedule

High temperatures break down engine oil faster. In Abu Dhabi, we recommend changing your oil every four to five thousand kilometres or every three months, whichever comes first. Using a high-quality synthetic oil is especially important in the UAE heat.

## 4. Test Your Battery Every Six Months

Car batteries degrade faster in hot climates. A battery that works in winter may fail completely in summer. Have your battery tested at least twice a year. If your battery is more than two years old, ask our team about a [car battery replacement in Abu Dhabi](/services/battery-replacement/).

## 5. Flush and Replace Coolant

The coolant system prevents your engine from overheating. In Abu Dhabi's heat, a degraded coolant can lead to costly engine damage. Check coolant levels monthly and have the full system flushed every two years.

## 6. Inspect Brake Pads and Discs

Abu Dhabi's stop-and-go traffic wears brakes quickly. Listen for squealing or grinding sounds and have your brakes inspected during every major service. Our [mechanical repairs team](/services/mechanical-repairs/) can assess and replace brake components for all car models.

## 7. Clean Air Filters Regularly

Desert dust clogs air filters faster than in other climates. A dirty air filter reduces engine performance and fuel economy. Inspect your air filter every three to four months and replace it when needed.

## 8. Check Wiper Blades and Washer Fluid

When it does rain in Abu Dhabi, roads become especially slippery due to settled dust and oil. Make sure your wiper blades are in good condition and your washer fluid reservoir is full.

## 9. Park in Shade When Possible

UV radiation and direct heat damage your car's paint, tyres, interior plastics, and leather. Park in covered parking whenever possible to extend the life of your vehicle. For paint protection and detailing, see our [Car Wash and Detailing service](/services/car-wash-detailing/).

## 10. Book an Annual Full Car Inspection

A full inspection covers all major systems — engine, brakes, AC, electrical, tyres, and chassis. At Al Sham Palace, we have been providing comprehensive vehicle inspections across all [Abu Dhabi service areas](/areas/) since 1999.
