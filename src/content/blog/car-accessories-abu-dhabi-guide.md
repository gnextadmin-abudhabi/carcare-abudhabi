Whether you have just bought a new car or want to upgrade your existing vehicle, the right accessories make driving in Abu Dhabi much more comfortable, safer, and enjoyable. At [Al Sham Palace Auto Car Service](/), we supply and install hundreds of accessories every month for Abu Dhabi drivers. Here are our top picks for 2026.

## 1. Dashboard Camera (Dashcam)

With busy Abu Dhabi roads and the occasional aggressive driver, a dashcam provides vital evidence in case of an accident. Modern dashcams record in 4K, have GPS tracking, and can record even when the car is parked.

**Best for**: All drivers, especially those who drive frequently on Emirates Road, Sheikh Khalifa Road, or around Mussafah.

## 2. Window Tinting Film

UAE law allows up to 30% light transmission on front windows and darker tint on rear windows. Quality tinting film reduces cabin temperature by up to 15°C, protects the interior from UV damage, and improves privacy.

At [Al Sham Palace](/services/car-accessories/), we fit quality ceramic and carbon tinting films that comply with UAE regulations.

## 3. Seat Covers

Abu Dhabi's heat turns leather seats into a burning surface. Quality seat covers protect the original upholstery, add comfort, and are much easier to clean. We stock covers for all car models including Toyota Land Cruiser, Nissan Patrol, GMC Yukon, and more.

## 4. Parking Sensors and Reversing Camera

Abu Dhabi's parking areas can be tight, and reversing accidents are common. Aftermarket parking sensors and reversing cameras are affordable, quick to install, and save significant repair costs. Our team installs these seamlessly with your existing system.

## 5. Car Sunshade (Windscreen Cover)

Leaving your car in the sun heats the interior to dangerous levels and causes dashboard cracking and fading. A good sunshade reduces interior temperature dramatically and protects the dashboard and steering wheel.

## 6. Car Floor Mats (Heavy Duty)

Standard carpet mats do not survive Abu Dhabi's sand and dust. Heavy-duty rubber or 3D moulded floor mats are easy to clean and protect the original carpet from staining and damage. We recommend 3D moulded mats that fit each car model precisely.

## 7. Steering Wheel Cover

Steering wheels get burning hot in the Abu Dhabi sun. A quality steering wheel cover in leather or microfiber insulates the heat and improves grip. We stock covers for all steering wheel sizes and styles.

## 8. USB Car Charger and Phone Mount

A quality phone mount and fast USB charger keeps you connected safely while driving. We recommend multi-port chargers with 65W fast charging for modern smartphones.

## 9. Car Fragrance and Air Purifier

Abu Dhabi's dust can create musty smells inside the car. A quality car air purifier with HEPA filter removes dust particles while a premium fragrance keeps the cabin fresh.

## 10. LED Ambient Interior Lighting

LED strip lighting inside the car adds a premium feel and improves visibility at night. Modern kits are easy to install and come with app control for colours and brightness.
